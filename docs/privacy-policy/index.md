# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 25/12/2025

## 1. Introduction

Al Ansar Masjid (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Al Ansar Masjid mobile application (the “App”).

This policy is written in accordance with the **Australian Privacy Act 1988** and the **Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)**.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

The App does **not require user accounts or login**.

We do **not** collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it, such as when:
- Making a donation
- Contacting us for support or enquiries

Personal information may include:
- Name (optional)
- Email address (optional)

---

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect limited technical information necessary for operation and security, including:
- Device type and operating system
- IP address
- App crash or error logs

This information is used **only for app functionality and stability**, not for advertising or user profiling.

---

## 3. Firebase Usage

The App uses **Firebase** to support core functionality such as:
- Secure data storage
- App stability and crash reporting (if enabled)

We **do not use Firebase Analytics**, advertising features, or cross-app tracking.

All data handled by Firebase is processed in accordance with **Google’s Privacy Policy** and secured using industry-standard protections.

---

## 4. Donations and Payments

The App allows users to make **voluntary donations**.

All payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers such as **[Stripe / Google Play / Apple App Store]**.

- We **do not collect or store payment card details**
- Payment providers handle payment information under their own privacy policies

---

## 5. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used only to:
- Operate and maintain the App
- Process donations
- Respond to user enquiries
- Improve app reliability and security
- Meet legal and regulatory obligations

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not sell, rent, or trade** personal information.

Information may be shared only:
- With service providers required to operate the App (e.g. Firebase, payment processors)
- Where required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the App

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to:
- Fulfil its intended purpose
- Meet legal or accounting requirements

When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

---

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:
- Secure servers and infrastructure
- Encrypted data transmission
- Restricted access controls

Despite these measures, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

The App is **not directed to children under 13 years of age**.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

---

## 10. Your Privacy Rights (Australia)

Under Australian law, you have the right to:
- Request access to personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Make a privacy complaint

Requests can be made using the contact details below.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Updates will be reflected in the App and by changing the “Effective Date”.

Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
